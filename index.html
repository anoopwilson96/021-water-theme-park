BIG RIVER

A blend of Fun & Adventure!

Left or right, wherever you look, you can hear giggles, screams of joy, and shouts of excitement! Big River is 30 acres of pure, fun-filled joyrides and adventure. With 50+ exhilarating rides that will leave you wanting for more, this is, without a doubt, the happiest place in India!

Big River is a place where memories are made, laughter fills the air, and the only limit to the fun is your imagination. So come on down, grab your swimsuit, and prepare to experience a world of aquatic adventures at Big River Water Theme Park!
